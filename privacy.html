Play Privacy Policy

Play is committed to protecting the privacy and accuracy of confidential information to the extent possible, subject to provisions of state and federal law. Other than as required by laws that guarantee public access to certain types of information, or in response to subpoenas or other legal instruments that authorize access, personal information is not actively shared. In particular, we do not re-distribute or sell personal information collected on our web servers.

Information collected:

Play servers collect the following analytics:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address of computer being used

  • web pages requested

  • referring web page

  • browser and operating system used

  • browser used

  • date and times accessing website and app

  • app usage

The Play website does not associate this data to individual user identities. The Play app associates app-specific usage data with the account you set up.

Cookies

Play site may use "cookies" in order to deliver web content specific to individual users' interests or to keep track of online purchasing transactions. Sensitive personal information is not stored within cookies.

Webforms

Play uses webforms forms on this site. These forms require users to give contact information. Contact information from the registration form is used only to send material relating to news about releases of the Play app for which it was collected and will not be sold to another party.

Use of collected information:

  • Play will only use personal information collected from this site or Play app for the purpose of communication back to individuals who contact us via the site or app.

  • Play uses browser-IP-address information and anonymous-browser history to report information about site accesses and for profiling purposes. This information is used to improve Web presentation and utilization. IP address information may also be used for troubleshooting purposes.

  • referring web page

Distribution of collected information:

  • Play will not disclose, without your consent, personal information collected about you, except for certain explicit circumstances in which disclosure is required by law.

  • Play will not distribute or sell personal information to third-party organizations.

Privacy Statement Revisions:

This Privacy Statement was last revised on Aug 31, 2023. We may change this Privacy Statement at any time and for any reason. We encourage you to review this Privacy Statement each time you visit the web site.

If we make a significant change to our Privacy Statement, we will post a notice on the homepage of our web site for a period of time after the change is made.

Responsibility for External Sites:

This website and the Play app may contain links to other web sites. Some of those web sites may be operated by third parties. We provide the links for your convenience, but we do not review, control, or monitor the privacy practices of web sites operated by others.

We are not responsible for the performance of web sites operated by third parties or for your business dealings with them. Therefore, whenever you leave this web site we recommend that you review each web site's privacy practices and make your own conclusions regarding the adequacy of these practices.
